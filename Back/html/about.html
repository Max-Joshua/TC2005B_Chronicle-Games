About 

Rumble of The Forest is about how the main character (Gordilla) overcome adversity through music and rhythm. It is a 2D, pixel art, platform and rhythm game.

The goal of the game is to get all the instruments to bring harmony and peace to the forest, using them to the beat of the music to activate their powers. 

The enemies will try to take out the main player throughout the levels by either wearing down the life bar by reaching him or using abilities to slow him down to prevent the protagonist from defeating the level boss in a musical duel.

Characters

Gordilla

A cute squirrel, master of the rhythm, determined to save the forest through the power of percussion. Enemy of Tenoch.

Miztli

Famous for hatting rodents more specifically, musical rodents. She will do anything to please Tenoch.

Tlacuatzin 

Even though he is the right hand of Tenoch, he really likes music, but he can’t rebel against his boss as that would get him killed.

Tenoch

 A very old and grumpy eagle. Enemy of the music, hates percussion instruments and seeks a silent and lonely forest, free from annoying animals and music.
